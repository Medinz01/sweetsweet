Title of the Project 
InstantB2C – Web Platform for Micro and Home-based B2C Sellers 
 
Software Requirements 
• Operating System (Server): Linux or Windows 10+ 
• Database: MySQL with Sequelize ORM 
• Frontend: Next.js (React) with Tailwind CSS & MUI 
• Backend: Node.js, Express.js (via Next.js API Routes), Python 
• Authentication: JOSE JWT-based Auth 
• AI Integration: Model Context Protocol (MCP) for intelligent bundling and 
seller prompts 
 
Hardware Requirements 
• Processor: Intel Core i5 or higher 
• RAM: 8 GB 
• Storage: 50 GB minimum 
 
Abstract 
InstantB2C is a Software as a Service (SaaS) web platform tailored to empower 
micro and small business owners—especially home-based entrepreneurs and social 
commerce sellers—to seamlessly set up and manage their own personalized online 
storefronts. Whether it's home-based bakers, sweet makers, or boutique sellers 
operating on platforms like Instagram or WhatsApp, InstantB2C offers a 
centralized, intuitive, and tech-free experience to list products, share store links, 
manage customer orders, and automate fulfillment workflows. 
This project reimagines traditional e-commerce by eliminating technical barriers, 
offering plug-and-play tools for sellers to establish and scale their digital presence 
in minutes. 
To further enhance business decision-making, InstantB2C integrates AI 
capabilities using the Model Context Protocol (MCP) — a standardized format for

Project Idea and Novelty 
InstantB2C addresses a major gap in the current e-commerce ecosystem: the lack 
of simple, intelligent digital tools for small and home-based sellers who operate 
primarily through social platforms like WhatsApp or Instagram. These sellers often 
face three main challenges: 
1. Lack of technical knowledge to build a store 
2. No access to real-time analytics or AI-based suggestions 
3. Manual, unscalable workflows (e.g., Excel sheets, DMs for orders) 
What Makes InstantB2C Novel? 
• Plug‑and‑play store creation: Sellers can launch a personalized online 
storefront in minutes—no coding or tech skills required. 
• Unified Operations Hub: Product listing, order tracking, inventory, 
promotions, and loyalty programs all in one dashboard. 
• On-the-go Business Intelligence:  
o AI-powered product bundle suggestions (e.g. sweets combos that 
typically sell together). 
o Real-time business prompts — “Add more of this flavor–it’s trending 
this week.” 
• No-Customer-Registration Checkout: Unlike large platforms, InstantB2C 
doesn’t require customers to create accounts, making it ideal for casual or 
one-time buyers common in home-based commerce. 
• Built-In Order Management & Discounts: Sellers can easily manage orders, 
track statuses, and apply promotions — all from a unified dashboard.

Conclusion 
InstantB2C is a complete digital storefront and operations platform designed for 
micro and home-based B2C sellers. With a no-code setup experience and a smooth 
customer-facing UI, it empowers traditional offline sellers to digitize their business 
with minimal effort. 
By integrating AI-driven insights using the Model Context Protocol (MCP), 
InstantB2C not only simplifies store setup and management, but also intelligently 
supports business growth through automated product bundling suggestions and 
data-driven selling prompts — making e-commerce more accessible and strategic 
for non-technical sellers.
